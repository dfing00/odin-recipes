Cheesy Lasagna

Hearty lasagna recipe with a ground beef meat sauce and three cheeses layered with uncooked lasagna noodles to save some work.

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 jar spaghetti sauce
  • cottage cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • parmesan cheese
  • parsley
  • salt and pepper
  • lasagna noodles

Steps

  1. In a large skillet over medium heat brown the ground beef. Drain the grease. Add spaghetti sauce and simmer for 5 minutes.
  2. In a large bowl, mix together the cottage cheese, 2 cups of the mozzerella cheese, eggs half of the grated Parmesan cheese, dried parlsey, salt and ground black pepper.
  3. to assemble, in the bottom of a 9x13 inch backing dish evenly spread 3/4 cup of the sauce mixture. Cover with 3 uncooked lasangna noodles,1 3/4 cup of the cheese mixture, and 1/4 cup sauce. Repeat layers once more: top with 3 noodle, remaining sauce, remaining mozzarella and Parmeasan cheese. Add 1/2 cup water to the edges of the pan. Cover with aluminum foil.
  4. Bake in a preheated 350 degree F(175 degree C) oven for 45 minutes. Uncover and bake an additional 10 minutes. Let stand 10 miuntes before serving.
